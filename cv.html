Short Academic Summary

Currently a Ph.D. Scholar at the TERI School of Advanced Studies, New Delhi, India. I hold an M.A. in Geography from Dr. B.R. Ambedkar University (Agra) and a B.A. (Honors) in Geography from Shaheed Bhagat Singh College, University of Delhi. My expertise includes remote sensing, GIS application development, cloud computing on Google Earth Engine, and applying machine learning to model environmental hazards.
